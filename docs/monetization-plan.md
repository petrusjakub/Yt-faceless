## YouTube Monetization Requirements

### YouTube Partner Program (YPP) Requirements

| Requirement | Target | Strategy |
|-------------|--------|----------|
| 1,000 Subscribers | Month 2-3 | Consistent uploads + SEO |
| 4,000 Watch Hours (12 months) | Month 2-3 | 15 videos/month x 10 min avg |
| OR 10M Shorts views (90 days) | Alternative | Repurpose long-form to Shorts |
| Follow YouTube policies | Always | No copyrighted content, proper disclaimers |

### Watch Hours Calculation

- 15 videos/month x 10 minutes average = 150 minutes of content/month
- If each video gets 500 views with 50% retention:
  - 500 views x 5 min watch = 2,500 minutes per video
  - 15 videos x 2,500 min = 37,500 minutes = **625 watch hours/month**
- **4,000 hours reached in ~6-7 months** (conservative)
- With viral videos and growth: **2-3 months** is achievable

## Revenue Optimization Strategies

### Maximizing AdSense RPM

1. **Longer videos (10-15 min)** - Place multiple mid-roll ads
2. **Mid-roll ad placement** - Every 3-4 minutes after the 8-minute mark
3. **Finance keywords in content** - Higher-paying ads get served
4. **Target US viewers** - US traffic has highest CPM
5. **Upload timing** - Publish when US audience is active (2-4 PM EST)

### Ad Placement Strategy (for 10-minute videos)

| Timestamp | Ad Type |
|-----------|---------|
| Pre-roll | Automatic (YouTube places this) |
| 3:00-3:30 | Mid-roll #1 (after first major point) |
| 6:00-6:30 | Mid-roll #2 (between sections) |
| 8:30-9:00 | Mid-roll #3 (before conclusion) |
| Post-roll | Automatic |

### Affiliate Marketing Best Practices

1. Only promote products you can genuinely recommend
2. Include affiliate links in EVERY video description
3. Mention the product naturally within content (not forced)
4. Create dedicated "review" or "tutorial" videos for high-commission products
5. Use link shorteners to track performance
6. Disclose affiliate relationships (FTC requirement)
