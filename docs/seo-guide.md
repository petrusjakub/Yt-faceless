# SEO Strategy Guide - WealthMind Academy

## YouTube SEO Fundamentals

YouTube is the world's second-largest search engine. Our strategy focuses on making every video discoverable through search, suggested videos, and browse features.

### The 3 Pillars of YouTube SEO

1. **Search Optimization** - Rank in YouTube and Google search results
2. **Engagement Signals** - CTR, watch time, likes, and comments
3. **Authority Building** - Channel consistency, niche relevance, upload frequency

## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Find Keywords

**Tools to Use:**
- YouTube Search Autocomplete (type topic, note suggestions)
- TubeBuddy Keyword Explorer
- VidIQ Keyword Inspector
- Google Trends (compare keyword interest)
- Ahrefs YouTube Keyword Tool (free limited version)

### Step 2: Evaluate Keywords

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Monthly Search Volume | 5,000+ |
| Competition Score | Low to Medium |
| Related Videos | Under 500K views average (room to compete) |
| Trend Direction | Stable or Growing |

## Title Optimization

### Title Formula Templates

1. **How-To**: "How to [ACHIEVE RESULT] ([TIMEFRAME/METHOD])"
   - Example: "How to Build Wealth in Your 20s (Step-by-Step Guide)"

2. **Number List**: "[NUMBER] [THINGS] That [PROMISE/RESULT]"
   - Example: "7 Money Habits That Will Make You Rich"

3. **VS/Comparison**: "[OPTION A] vs [OPTION B]: Which is [BETTER/RIGHT] For You?"
   - Example: "Roth IRA vs 401k: Which Should You Choose?"

4. **Mistake/Warning**: "[NUMBER] [TOPIC] Mistakes That [NEGATIVE OUTCOME]"
   - Example: "5 Investing Mistakes That Keep You Poor"

5. **Shocking Fact**: "Why [COMMON BELIEF] is Wrong ([REAL TRUTH])"
   - Example: "Why Saving Money Won't Make You Rich (Do This Instead)"

6. **Blueprint/Guide**: "The Complete [TOPIC] Guide for [AUDIENCE]"
   - Example: "The Complete Beginner's Guide to Index Fund Investing"

### Title Rules

- Keep under 60 characters (full visibility on mobile)
- Put the primary keyword in the first half
- Use numbers when possible (specific > vague)
- Include a benefit or curiosity gap
- Avoid ALL CAPS (looks spammy)
- Test with the question: "Would I click this?"

## Tag Strategy

### Tag Categories (Use 10-15 per video)

1. **Primary keyword** (exact match) - Always first
2. **Primary keyword variations** (2-3 tags)
3. **Secondary keywords** (2-3 tags)
4. **Broad niche tags** (2-3 tags)
5. **Channel brand tag** (always include)
6. **Competitor channel names** (1-2 tags)

### Tag Examples for "Index Funds for Beginners"

```
index funds for beginners
how to invest in index funds
index fund investing
best index funds 2024
S&P 500 index fund
vanguard index funds
index funds vs mutual funds
investing for beginners
how to start investing
personal finance
WealthMind Academy
passive investing
stock market for beginners
```

### Tag Rules
- First tag = exact primary keyword
- Total tags should use 400-500 characters (max)
- Mix specific and broad tags
- Always include channel name as a tag
- Update tags monthly based on trending searches

## YouTube Shorts SEO

### Shorts Strategy (Supplement to Long-Form)

- Keep titles under 40 characters
- Use 3-5 hashtags: #shorts #finance #investing #[topic]
- First frame must grab attention
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Repurpose best moments from long-form videos

---

## Algorithm Signals to Optimize

| Signal | What YouTube Measures | How to Optimize |
|--------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Click-Through Rate | % of impressions that become views | Better thumbnails + titles |
| Watch Time | Total minutes watched | Better content, longer videos |
| Average View Duration | % of video watched | Strong hooks, good pacing |
| Engagement | Likes, comments, shares | Ask questions, CTA |
| Session Time | Do viewers watch MORE YouTube after? | End screens, cards, playlists |
| Upload Consistency | Regular publishing schedule | Every other day, same time |

## Playlist Strategy

### Create These Playlists (Day 1)

1. **Investing for Complete Beginners** - Entry-level investing content
2. **Money Management Masterclass** - Budgeting and saving
3. **Passive Income Strategies** - All passive income content
4. **Wealth Psychology** - Mindset and habits content
5. **Quick Tips (Under 5 Minutes)** - Shorter educational clips

### Playlist SEO
- Playlist titles should include keywords
- Descriptions should be 100+ words with keywords
- Order videos logically (beginner to advanced)
- Add new videos to relevant playlists immediately

## Monthly SEO Audit Checklist

- [ ] Review top 5 performing videos - what keywords are they ranking for?
- [ ] Check YouTube Analytics > Traffic Sources > YouTube Search
- [ ] Identify new keyword opportunities from search terms
- [ ] Update descriptions/tags on underperforming videos
- [ ] Check competitor channels for new trending topics
- [ ] Review CTR for all videos - update thumbnails for below 4%
- [ ] Add new videos to appropriate playlists
- [ ] Update channel keywords based on performance data
