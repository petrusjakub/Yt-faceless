## Why This Niche?

The Personal Finance niche offers some of the highest CPMs on YouTube ($15-$40 per 1,000 views) because advertisers in banking, investing, insurance, and fintech pay premium rates to reach this audience. Combined with a faceless format and AI-powered production, this creates a scalable, low-overhead content business.

## Tools Used

- **NotebookLM Studio Video** - AI-powered research and script generation
- **AI Studio Google** - AI narration, visuals, and video assembly
- **Canva / Photopea** - Thumbnail creation
- **TubeBuddy / VidIQ** - SEO research and optimization
